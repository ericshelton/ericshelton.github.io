Eric Shelton

Eric Shelton is a designer, strategist, and photographer based in Philly. Go birds! He graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design from The University of the Arts (RIP).

His design work is research and strategy-driven, and thrives on collaboration. It has been developed with multidisciplinary teams of digital gurus (yuck), strategists, architects, designers, researchers, writers, etc.

Eric’s most recent projects are focused on developing UX design and strategy for FAS at Vanguard. He focuses on creating intuitive financial tools for advisors and their clients. Through his design thinking, has has always prioritized simplicity and clarity, which helps with these incredibly complex tools for financial planning.

He also wonders why these pages are always written in third person (like he’s done here).
